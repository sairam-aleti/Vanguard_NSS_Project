About Vanguard

Pioneering Secure Logistics Since 1987

What started as a single freight forwarding office in Rotterdam has grown into a global logistics powerhouse, serving over 3,000 enterprise clients across 150+ countries.

Our Journey

37 Years of Innovation

1987

Founded in Rotterdam

Hans van der Berg establishes Vanguard Freight Services with a single office and three employees, specializing in European ground transport.

1995

Ocean Freight Expansion

Partnership with major shipping lines enables FCL/LCL services across Asia-Pacific routes. Revenue crosses $100M for the first time.

2003

Air Freight Division

Launch of dedicated air freight operations with charter capabilities. Opens regional hubs in Singapore, Dubai, and Chicago.

2012

Digital Transformation

Launch of proprietary tracking platform with real-time GPS visibility. Becomes first logistics firm to achieve ISO 27001 for information security.

2019

Zero-Trust Architecture

Implementation of enterprise-wide zero-trust security across all IT infrastructure. SOC 2 Type II certification achieved.

2024

AI-Powered Logistics

Deployment of machine learning models for route optimization, demand forecasting, and automated customs documentation. Revenue exceeds $2.8B.
